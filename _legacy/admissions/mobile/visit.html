Visit Us

Located on the North Shore of Long Island, 60 miles east of New York City, Stony Brook's 1,000-acre campus is in a beautiful, natural setting close to beaches and woodlands. And, with a Long Island Rail Road station right on campus, travling from New York City and JFK Airport couldn't be easier.

During the academic year, student-guided campus tours are offered on most weekdays and on Saturdays. Information sessions, hosted by admissions counselors, are offered on Saturdays. During the summer, campus tours and information sessions are hosted on most weekdays.
