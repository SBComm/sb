Now that you are actively involved in the college selection process, I would like to take a few moments to familiarize you with Stony Brook University.

  • Stony Brook is ranked among the top 1% percent of universities in the world by the London Times Higher Education World University Rankings. 

  • Our undergraduates enjoy countless opportunities for students to participate in cutting edge research and creative projects alongside our award-winning faculty. 

  • We offer more than 200 majors, minors, and combined-degree programs. 

  • Our tuition is amongst the lowest of any comparable university.
