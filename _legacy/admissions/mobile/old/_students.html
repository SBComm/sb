Student Life

At Stony Brook, you'll find a blend of people, activities, interest groups, and sports that will take your college experience far beyond the academic. The fun begins with Opening Week and is sustained throughout the year with such events as Homecoming, Midnight Madness, the Roth Regatta, and the Strawberry Festival. A wide variety of lectures, seminars, concerts, exhibits, theatrical performances, movies, and sporting events are also scheduled regularly throughout the academic year.
