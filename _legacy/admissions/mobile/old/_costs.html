Financial Aid & Scholarships
We offer both merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid awards. The majority of our merit scholarships require no special applications; all admitted students who are academically qualified are automatically considered for merit aid.

Our Office of Financial Aid and Scholarship Services administers three basic categories of need-based aid: loans, employment opportunities, and grants. To apply for federal and New York State aid, complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). Stony Brook's FAFSA code is 002838.

Upon submitting the FAFSA, New York State residents will be directed to the Express Tuition Assistant Program (TAP) Application for New York State assistance and scholarship programs.
