Why SB

Stony Brook University ranks among the Top 1% universities in the world according to the London Times Higher Education World Rankings. We are also called a "Best Buy" by the Fiske Guide to Colleges.

Here are a few more reasons why we should be the first choice for your education. We offer:

  • A top tier education at just a fraction of the cost of a private university.
  • Prime location on Long Island’s North Shore, midway between Montauk and New York City.
  • An award-winning undergraduate research program.
  • One of the nation’s top university-based teaching hospitals.
  • One-of-a-kind study abroad opportunities.
  • Internships to blast you to the front of the line.
  • Hundreds of clubs and organizations on campus—and the beach, parks, and shopping areas only a short ride away. You won’t have to go far to find fun.
